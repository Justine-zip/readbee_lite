readbee_lite

readbee_lite is a mobile application designed to assess and enhance students’ reading comprehension skills. It provides a structured and interactive environment where learners can practice reading, answer comprehension questions, and track their progress over time.

The app aims to eliminate common reading struggles, helping students gain confidence and fluency across multiple languages. With curated reading materials, quizzes, and guided exercises, readbee_lite supports educators in monitoring performance while fostering independent learning.

Core Features

Digital Evaluation – Assess students’ reading comprehension through interactive exercises.

Automatic Calculation of Session Results – Scores and performance metrics are calculated instantly.

Accurate Miscue Detection – Identify reading errors precisely to provide meaningful feedback.

Progress Tracking – Monitor improvement over time for individual students or groups.

Multi-language Support – Practice reading and comprehension in different languages.

Interactive Quizzes – Engaging questions to reinforce learning and comprehension skills.

User-friendly Interface – Easy navigation for both students and educators.

Whether for classroom use or at-home practice, readbee_lite encourages students to develop critical reading skills, improve vocabulary, and build comprehension strategies in an engaging and efficient way.
